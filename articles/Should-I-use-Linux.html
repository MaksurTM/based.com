Linux

It's a bazar

GNU/Linux is not your average operating system, if you are looking for a replacement for Windows or Macintosh you've come to the wrong place. The Linux experience is something else entirely, it is a bazar instead of a palace/walled garden. Instead of being a singular thing, one set of apps built by one mega corporation, your linux installation is always a unique configuration of different programs working together, programs that you chose, installed and organized. Open and free programs that do one thing and do it well, that is the UNIX philosophy. An operating system that you have total control over. There is nothing you can't modify or change or swap, Linux is free as in freedom and as in free beer!

Why would I need this?

As I said before, it is not a replacement for Windows or Mac. When you install Linux, you can do it because of one of many reasons:
